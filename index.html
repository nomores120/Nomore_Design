What is Bullying?

Bullying is the repeated and intentional behavior of harming or intimidating someone, often with the intention of asserting power or control over them.

Types of Bullying

  • Verbal Bullying
  • Physical Bullying
  • Cyberbullying
  • Relational Bullying

Effects of Bullying

Bullying can have serious and long-lasting effects on the victim, including anxiety, depression, and even suicidal thoughts.

How to Stop Bullying

We can stop bullying by promoting empathy, kindness, and respect for all individuals. We can also report incidents of bullying to authorities and support those who are affected.
